(02)Getting There
⚠ Traffic Notice — US 75 Overnight Closures

Coming up from Dallas? Give yourself extra time.

The City of Richardson has overnight closures on US 75 near Renner Road (just south of the President George Bush Turnpike) for DART Silver Line bridge work, July 23–26.

The part that affects our start: the Friday night closure runs until 6 a.m. Saturday and shuts both northbound and southbound US 75 main lanes near Renner Road. Since we roll out at 5:30 a.m., our Dallas crew driving up 75 from the south will still be inside that window.

The plan: Punch Towne Lake into Google Maps or Waze right before you leave. It reroutes around whatever's actually closed that morning, so let it do the thinking. Give yourself an extra 10 to 15 minutes. If you hit the closure near Renner Rd, the frontage roads are the usual way around.

Mainly a heads-up for the Dallas folks coming up 75 from the south. Coming from another direction? You're probably in the clear. Schedule is subject to change with weather or construction. Details from the City of Richardson (cor.net).
